Movie Poster, Roxy is Okay

September 2019

Movie poster created for the short film, Roxy is Okay, a submission to the San Francisco 48 Hour Film Project.

Roxy is Okay is a mockumentary about an aspiring voice actress working at a small game production company, and draws inspiration from shows like The Office. The poster was designed to highlight the satirical nature of the film and the interactions between the characters.

Software: Adobe Photoshop and Illustrator.
