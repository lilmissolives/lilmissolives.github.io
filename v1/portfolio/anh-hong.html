Anh Hong Express Logo

August 2017

Created for Anh Hong restaurant in San Francisco, for their take-out subdivision launched in 2017.

Since the restaurant owners no longer had a digital copy of their logo, I recreated the cow graphic and incorporated it into a modern logo that expresses the restaurant's entrance into the take-out and delivery business.
